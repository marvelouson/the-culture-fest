Our Rides

There are three types of rides in The Culture Fest. We can provide you with thrill rides, family rides and kiddie rides. We has a total of 25 rides such as the Ferris Wheel, Vortex, Sky Rider, Merry Go Round, Dodgem Car, Top Gun, Haunted Castle and many more.

All of our rides are imported from Europe and are checked reqularly according to Malaysia DOSH standards. Our rides are reqularly maintained and inspected prior opening in each venue. Being the largest travelling fun park in Malaysia, everyone safety is out primary concern. All our staffs are well-trained to serve you better.
